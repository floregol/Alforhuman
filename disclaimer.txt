The experiment is divided in three part.

1) Initially, you will be showed some of the images and their category.
2) Then, you will be asked to categorize a few images and you will get instant feedback on if your categorization was right.
3) The last step is the testing phase, you will be asked to categorize some images and feedback won't be provided.
