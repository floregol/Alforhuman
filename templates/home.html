Welcome to this new active learning experiment

Disclaimer

If you agree to proceed, you will participate in an experiment in which you will be asked to look at images and to categorize them.

The categorization task is based on a rule that you will have to infer based on the examples that will be shown to you. The experiment is divided in three parts.

1) Initially, you will be shown some of the images and their associated categories, marked by either 0 or 1.

2) Then, you will be asked to categorize some new images. You will receive feedback on wether or not you chose the right category for each image.

During those first 2 phases, you should try to infer the rule based on the feedback you receive.

3) The last step is the testing phase, you will again be asked to categorize some images but feedback won't be provided.

Requirements

Participants are required to visually distinguish images, it is not suitable for colorblind people.

Data Privacy

No user-related information is collected, the record of the experiment is stored in a private server. If you have any questions about the research/data/other, you can contact florence.robert-regol[at]mail.mcgill.ca

By clicking on 'Start the experiment', you agree to participate in the experiment and consent to the recording and storage of your data as part of this experiment.
